Government Agricultural Schemes

Discover financial aid, subsidies, and insurance policies designed to support and protect farmers.

💰

PM-KISAN Yojana

Direct Income Support

Overview

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central sector scheme that provides income support to all landholding farmer families in the country.

Eligibility

  • All landholding farmer families.
  • Must have a valid Aadhaar card and bank account.

Benefits

Financial benefit of ₹6,000 per year, released in three equal installments of ₹2,000 directly to bank accounts.

Apply on PM-KISAN Portal →

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Credit Facility

Overview

Provides farmers with timely access to adequate credit for agricultural expenses at heavily subsidized interest rates.

Eligibility

  • Individual farmers, joint borrowers, and tenant farmers.
  • Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Joint Liability Groups (JLGs).

Benefits

Flexible loan repayment options, low interest rates (as low as 4% with prompt repayment), and simplified disbursement processes.

PKVY (Organic Farming)

Financial Assistance

Overview

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana aims to promote organic farming through cluster approach and Participatory Guarantee System (PGS) certification.

Eligibility

  • Farmers willing to adopt chemical-free organic farming.
  • Groups of 50 or more farmers forming a cluster of 50 acres.

Benefits

Financial assistance of ₹50,000 per hectare for 3 years, covering seeds, bio-fertilizers, and harvesting logistics.

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop Insurance

Overview

A comprehensive crop insurance scheme designed to provide financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events.

Eligibility

  • Farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.
  • Includes sharecroppers and tenant farmers.

Benefits

Extremely low premium rates (2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops). Full insured amount paid against crop failures due to natural calamities.

Apply on PMFBY Portal →

PM KUSUM Yojana

Solar Energy

Overview

Aims to provide energy and water security to farmers by setting up standalone solar pumps and solarizing grid-connected agriculture pumps.

Eligibility

  • Individual farmers, Water User Associations, and FPOs.

Benefits

Up to 60% subsidy on the cost of standalone solar pumps. Farmers can also sell surplus solar power back to the grid for extra income.

Apply on PM KUSUM Portal →

National Agri Market (e-NAM)

Digital Market

Overview

Pan-India electronic trading portal which networks existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Eligibility

  • Any registered farmer, trader, or FPO.

Benefits

Provides better price discovery through transparent auction processes and gives access to a wider market beyond local mandis.

Register on e-NAM →

Soil Health Card Scheme

Resource Management

Overview

Provides farmers with soil nutrient status of their holdings and advises them on the dosage of fertilizers to maintain soil health.

Eligibility

  • All farmers in India are eligible to get their soil tested.

Benefits

Helps in reducing the cost of cultivation by optimizing fertilizer application. Promotes sustainable farming and higher yields.

PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Irrigation Support

Overview

Aims to improve farm water use efficiency and expand cultivable area under assured irrigation ("Har Khet Ko Pani").

Eligibility

  • Farmers owning agricultural land.
  • Preference given to small and marginal farmers.

Benefits

Heavy subsidies on installing micro-irrigation systems (Drip and Sprinkler systems) to maximize water conservation.

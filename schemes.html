Government Schemes (Sarkari Yojanayein)

Explore subsidies, financial support, and agricultural schemes provided by the government for farmer welfare.

Financial Support

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

An initiative by the Government of India in which all farmers will get up to ₹6,000 per year as minimum income support.

Benefit: ₹6,000/year directly to bank account in 3 equal installments.
Official Website ↗
Insurance

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Provides a comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the crop thus helping in stabilising the income of the farmers.

Benefit: Extremely low premium (1.5% - 2%) for maximum crop loss coverage due to natural calamities.
Official Website ↗
Credit / Loan

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Aims to provide adequate and timely credit support from the banking system to the farmers for their cultivation and agricultural needs.

Benefit: Short-term loans at a very low interest rate of 4% (on prompt repayment).
More Info ↗
Equipment / Power

PM-KUSUM Yojana

Focuses on ensuring energy security for farmers in India, along with honoring India's commitment to increase the share of installed capacity of electric power.

Benefit: Up to 60% subsidy for setting up solar agriculture pumps and grid-connected solar power plants.
Official Website ↗
Soil Health

Soil Health Card Scheme

The scheme aims at promoting soil test based and balanced use of fertilisers to enable farmers to realize higher yields at lower cost.

Benefit: Free soil testing and a personalized report detailing required nutrients (NPK) for your specific field.
Official Website ↗
Organic Farming

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas (PKVY)

An elaborated component of Soil Health Management (SHM) of major project National Mission of Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA).

Benefit: Financial assistance of ₹50,000 per hectare for 3 years to adopt organic farming and certification.

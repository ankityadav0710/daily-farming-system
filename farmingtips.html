Modern Farming Tips

Adopt these proven agricultural practices and techniques to enhance your crop yield, improve soil health, and save resources.

Pre-Sowing

1. Soil Testing (Mitti Parikshan)

Always test your soil before planting a new crop. It helps you understand exactly which nutrients are missing (NPK) and saves money by preventing excess fertilizer use.

Water Management

2. Drip & Sprinkler Irrigation

Avoid flood irrigation. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to the roots, saving up to 50% water and reducing weed growth significantly.

Soil Health

3. Crop Rotation

Do not grow the same crop (like Wheat-Rice) continuously. Introduce leguminous crops (pulses) in between to naturally restore Nitrogen in the soil.

Organic Farming

4. Vermicomposting

Use earthworms to turn farm waste and cow dung into high-quality organic compost. It improves soil texture and increases water holding capacity.

Disease Prevention

5. Seed Treatment

Before sowing, treat your seeds with Trichoderma or Carbendazim. This simple step protects early seedlings from root rot and soil-borne diseases.

Pest Control

6. Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

Don't just rely on chemicals. Use Neem oil spray, pheromone traps, and friendly insects (like ladybugs) to control harmful pests naturally.
